我的作品

自主學習

高中二年級

作品連結

高二時的自主學習計畫「NBA專題報導撰寫」，因為我很喜歡籃球，所以決定以此為出發點做一份:《NBA2019-2020賽季湖人隊奪冠之旅》的報導。期間我學到了很多，除了自己製作封面、排版、文案撰寫，最重要的是我還學會了與人溝通、共同解決問題的能力。

Because I really like basketball, I decided to do a report about the NBA 2019-2020 Lakers' championship run in my senior year of high school. I learned a lot during the project. Besides making my own cover, layout, copywriting, and most importantly, I learned to communicate with people and solve problems together.

多媒體音樂

高中二年級

作品連結

多媒體音樂課程製作potcast「暖氣團」 主要負責主持和協助剪輯。第一次製作potcast的經驗令我非常印象深刻，課程才進行不到一半就遭遇疫情來襲，整個potcast是我們小組在家分工遠端完成的。由於錄製的過程是線上群組通話所以也是十分艱辛，盡管成果差強人意，值得慶幸的是我們最後還是完成了。

I was mainly responsible for hosting and helping with the editing of the potcast I produced for the multimedia music program "The Warm Air Group". I was very impressed with my first potcast experience. The class was not even halfway through when the pandemic hit and the whole potcast was done remotely by our group at home. The recording process was very difficult because it was an online group call, and even though the results were not good, we thankfully finished it in the end.

影片製作

高中二年級

作品連結

代表內壢高中參加青年城市論壇，活動期間我們拍攝了影片，內容為用較輕鬆幽默的方式隨機街訪攤販，我的工作主要是主持、剪輯音檔和上字幕。雖然完成品呈現的方式有點滑稽，但仍然是很寶貴的經驗。

During the event, we filmed a video of random street vendors in a light-hearted and humorous way, and my job was mainly to host, edit the audio and put up the subtitles. Although the finished product was a bit funny, it was still a very valuable experience.

直播節目

大學一年級

作品連結

大一錄製的直播節目，我擔任的是幕後的角色，負責操作直播串流軟體obs。操作obs除了要和導播機溝通配合好鏡位切換，我也負責控制直播中的開場影片、中場影片、直播開始前的字卡、直播結束後的字卡和節目中各單元的字卡的轉場，是十分重要的工作。

I was behind the scenes of a live show recorded in my freshman year of college, and I was responsible for operating the live streaming software, obs. This is a very important job for a live show

線上策展

大學一年級

作品連結

大一的數位內容策展課程期末作業，策展的主題是「景美夜市美食推薦: 鵝媽媽切仔麵」。我負責的工作是組長，工作內容主要是負責企畫構想和協助製作，我覺得這次的作品成果不盡理想，仍有很多可以進步的空間。

For the final assignment of the digital content curation course in my freshman year, the theme of the curation was "Jingmei Night Market Food Recommendation: Goose Mother Cut Noodles". I was the team leader of the project, and I was mainly responsible for conceptualizing and assisting in the production of the project. I think the result of this project is not perfect, and there is still a lot of room for improvement.

網頁製作

大學一年級

作品連結

我做的第一個網頁，雖然是套模板完成的，但仍然是我的心血。就算只是套模板而已我還是花了很多心力跟時間，也學到了很多，但那也只是皮毛而已，畢竟學海無涯，唯勤是途。

I made the first web page, although it is a set of templates to complete, but is still my blood and sweat. Even if it is just a template, I still spent a lot of effort and time, but also learned a lot, but that is only the skin, after all, the sea of learning is endless, only diligence is the way.
